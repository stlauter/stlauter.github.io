High Regards

April 16, 2026

How much more prolific,
of a writer,
I could become?

If only advanced by age.

I searched longingly
for some poetry to indulge in,
and what I found wasn't satisfactory.

These poets, albeit literate and eloquent,
lacked abstraction—
lacking the romance that writing brings.

Rooted solely in events,
poetry inspired by experience,
rather than expression of misunderstanding.

And I found this to be disappointing.

Such writers are heralded as great—
who acclaimed them?

Perhaps I am lacking
in the richness of my perception,
but these writers of acclaim fall short;

of my artistic glory.
